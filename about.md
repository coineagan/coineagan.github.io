Hi, I'm Sean Cunningham.

Coineagan is the Gaelic version of Cunningham. A much better name, I think. Less common. Less ham.

I live in Japan on a sub-tropical island in a small apartment surrounded by pit viper infested jungles.

I'm a web developer of sorts. More of a keeper of an app graveyard, really.

I created this website to house my writing. For selfish reasons, I post some of my writing on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/coineagan) and [Medium](https://medium.com/@coineagan).

This site is built on [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/). I don’t use ads, analytics, affiliate links, or trackers of any kind.
